Security by Default

Some of your most personal moments are shared on
WhatsApp, which is why we built end-to-end encryption into
the latest versions of our app. When end-to-end encrypted,
your messages and calls are secured so only you and the
person you're communicating with can read or listen to them,
and nobody in between, not even WhatsApp.
